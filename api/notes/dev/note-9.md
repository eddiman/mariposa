**Ingredients:**

- 400g spaghetti
- 200g guanciale
- 4 egg yolks
- 100g pecorino romano
- Black pepper

**Instructions:**

1. Cook pasta al dente
2. Fry guanciale until crispy
3. Mix eggs with cheese
4. Combine everything off heat
